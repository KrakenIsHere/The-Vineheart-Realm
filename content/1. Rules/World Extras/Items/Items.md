# Items

A running list of the magic items that turn up in the realm.

## Bag of Holding
[[DM Items#Bag of Holding|DM Extras]]

A sturdy sack that's bigger on the inside. It holds way more than it should (up to 500 lbs (about 227 kg), and about 64 cubic feet of room) while only ever weighing around 15 lbs (about 7 kg) from the outside. Reach in, think of the thing you want, and it's in your hand. Anything alive shut inside only gets about 10 minutes of air, and if the Bag gets torn or stuffed past its limit it can rupture and dump everything into the space between spaces.

## Ring of Three Wishes
[[DM Items#Ring of Three Wishes|DM Extras]]

A plain-looking ring that holds exactly three Wishes, no more. Make the wish, and one of the three is spent. When the last one's gone it's just a ring, though nobody wearing it quite believes that until they've tried. As far as getting a Wish granted goes this is about the most honest source in the realm, it does roughly what you ask.

## Luck Blade
[[DM Items#Luck Blade|DM Extras]]

A sword that's just plain lucky to have on you (a little bump to your saves while you're holding it), with somewhere between one and four Wishes tucked away in the pommel. You never know how many are left until you're out.

## Efreeti Bottle
[[DM Items#Efreeti Bottle|DM Extras]]

A heavy brass bottle with an Efreeti sealed inside. Let it out and it can grant up to three Wishes, though an Efreeti is a proud and spiteful thing, and you word a Wish carefully around one or not at all.

## Well of Many Worlds
[[DM Items#Well of Many Worlds|DM Extras]]

A square of black cloth that opens a door to somewhere else entirely when you unfold it, another land or another plane, and you don't know which until you're staring through it. Mostly it's a gate to wherever it feels like taking you.
