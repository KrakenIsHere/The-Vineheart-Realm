# The Catalyst

Farmer Hendrik's pig, Lady Truffles, won the harvest festival's "Finest Swine" competition. Farmer Willem, Hendrik's brother-in-law, congratulated him but accidentally said the pig was "almost as lovely as my Rosalind" (his own pig). Hendrik heard it as "almost as lovely as my Rosalind" (Willem's wife, who was Hendrik's sister). The ensuing argument about whether Willem had complimented the pig or insulted his wife split the family, then the village.
