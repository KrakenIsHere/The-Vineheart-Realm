# The Misty Falls
A cave-and-ruin complex beneath heavy mist, known from early BGW records.

## Notable Features
- Hidden descent routes into old stone chambers
- Residual warding magic and mimic activity

## Adventure Hook
The site is being reoccupied by treasure hunters who ignore old warnings.
