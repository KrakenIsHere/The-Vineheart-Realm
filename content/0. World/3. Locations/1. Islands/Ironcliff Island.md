# Ironcliff Island
A fortified island outpost built around iron-rich cliffs and naval batteries.

## Notable Features
- Shipyard dry docks and chain barriers
- Cliffside lift routes to foundries

## Adventure Hook
Saboteurs are cutting anchor chains before a military convoy departs.
