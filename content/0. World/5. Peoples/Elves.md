# Elves

> [!QUOTE] Core concept
> The **past** is not gone. We walk it.

## Memory made present
Elves do not sleep; they [[Character Creation#Elf|Trance]], and in trance they relive their own memories with all their original colour. To an Elf, a joy from two centuries ago is not a faded story; it is a place they can return to tonight. So is a grief. So is a betrayal.

This is why Elves seem unable to "move on." They are not brooding; they are simply still *there*, in a moment others let fade long ago. A debt owed to an Elf's grandmother may still, to the Elf who watched it made, feel owed this week.

## Kindreds
- **High Elves** keep the memory of cities, lore, and the old arts.
- **Wood Elves** keep the memory of the living wild, and are common in the deep forests of [[Arcanum]] and eastern [[Onyxia]].
- **Drow**, the elves who went below during the [[Divine War]] and stayed, relive not the world's memory but the *bloodline's*, ruled by matriarchs who hold the longest memories, and wary of a sunlit surface that forgets so easily.

> [!TIP] Friction
> The short-lived races find Elves aloof and slow to forgive. From the Elf's side, others simply *forget* too fast, treating the dead as gone and old wrongs as settled, when to the Elf nothing has faded at all.
