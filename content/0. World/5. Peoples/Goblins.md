# Goblins

Goblin culture doesn't have a concept of "Property". A stick on the ground and a tool in a locked shed are equally up for grabs if a thing needs doing. They'll casually take and leave things all over their communities, eat from communal pots, and genuinely Do Not Understand why the [[Peoples#The Core Races|Core Races]] are so Angry and prone to Violence all the time.

Consequently Goblins who live near Core communities develop a reputation as "Thieves", despite not even having a *word* for that. (The closest word they have is more like "Greedy", and it means a person who hides things so nobody else can use them, and it's a surefire fight-starter to call a Goblin that.)
