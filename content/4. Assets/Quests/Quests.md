# Active Quest Board

## Dragon Cave (Arcanum)
A dragon is rumored trapped in a cave south of Dickey on [[Arcanum]].
- Objective: verify if the creature is trapped, dead, or nesting.
- Reward: 200 gp and one faction favor from local officials.

## Abandoned Excavation Site
An old excavation has gone silent after tremors and strange lights.
- Objective: recover records, identify hazards, and secure survivors if any.
- Reward: salvage rights on one recovered artifact (DM approval).

## Ritual Supply Escort
The [[The Godwounded|Wounded]] request escort for ritual supplies before the next Binding.
- Objective: secure shipment from port to temple and identify saboteurs.
- Reward: +1 reputation with The Wounded and temple stipend.

## Smugglers of Ironshore
Illegal cargo routes around [[Ironshore]] are disrupting inter-continental trade.
- Objective: trace the route and expose the sponsor.
- Reward: bounty payout and port authority access.

## The Fallowfield Mediation
The feud in [[Fallowfield]] risks reigniting despite previous interventions.
- Objective: prevent violence during a shared seasonal festival.
- Reward: dual village patronage and lodging rights.

## Verdant Disturbance
The [[Verdant Network]] reports aggressive growth patterns near a recovery site.
- Objective: investigate whether the disturbance is natural, planar, or Godwound-related.
- Reward: nature cache and +1 Verdant reputation.

## Consortium Breach
The [[Ethereal Consortium]] lost contact with a survey team near a planar anomaly.
- Objective: recover team members and stabilize the breach.
- Reward: planar ward token and guild standing.
