# Half-Orcs

> [!QUOTE] Core concept
> You are who **raised** you, not who bore you.

## The kept
[[Orcs|Orc]] belief already holds that all Children are the responsibility of the Whole Community, whoever bore them, and that a lost child, once fed, is simply *kept*. Many Half-Orcs come up through exactly this: claimed without a second thought by an Orc community, or else raised in Core society bearing marks that set them apart.

Either way, a Half-Orc learns early that **family is made, not inherited**, and tends to take the lesson harder than almost anyone.

## Between two creeds
Raised among Orcs, a Half-Orc carries the belief that the [[Orcs|Soul grows through unasked Challenge]] into Core towns that would rather just *help* them, and often goes looking for hardship anyway, to Grow. Raised among the Core Races, they bring a blunt, communal warmth into places that find it strange.

> [!TIP] Friction
> Claimed by both worlds and fully at home in neither, Half-Orcs are forever being told they're "really" one thing or the other. Most have decided the question is nonsense: they are who fed them.
