# Gnomes

> [!QUOTE] Core concept
> To stop **asking** is to start dying.

## Curiosity as life
A Gnome may live four or five centuries, and to a Gnome the whole point of so much time is to never run out of **surprise**. Boredom is treated almost as an illness; an unanswered question is a small debt owed to the world. They begin projects they cannot possibly finish, purely for the joy of the asking.

**Rock** gnomes aim their curiosity at made things: clockwork, tinkering, how it all fits together. **Forest** gnomes aim it at living things: illusions, small beasts, the secrets of green places.

## Where the questions lead
Gnomes are over-represented in [[Memberia|Memberia's]] [[Shieldhaven|Athenaeum]], where curiosity is currency, and where their appetite for *any* answer, even a dangerous one, sometimes leads them toward knowledge better left sealed, such as the fragments of the [[The Immortal Soul|Immortal Soul]] legend quarantined in its lower stacks.

> [!TIP] Friction
> Others find Gnomes flighty, forever chasing the next question, rarely finishing the last. But a Gnome who has stopped asking has, by their own lights, already begun to die.
